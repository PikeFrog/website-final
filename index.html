What I do

Model & Rig Creation

I create custom models using Autodesk Maya and MudBox. I create a corresponding rig to go with these models. Animations are then keyframed off of these rigs to show their full flexibility.

VR/AR Development

I populate scenes with in Unity using prefabs which I create in Maya. In concurrence with these prefabs I build custom scripts to have these game objects work and flow as intended.

UI/UX Design

I make sure that every page and every step a user will experience in their interaction with the finished product will conform to the overall vision which has been formulated.

Avian Character Rig

This character was created starting from a cube. The body was then proportioned out in Maya before being brought into Mudbox to begin the sculpting process. Following the sculpting of the anatomy of the body I began to sculpt in the armor pieces of the model. After the armor was sculpted onto the mesh I began the texturing process in Mudbox. The last process was the rigging which took place in Maya along with a simple walk cycle to test the rig.
